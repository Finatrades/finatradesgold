COMPLETE FINATRADES GUIDE
Step-by-Step Instructions for Every Feature
1. WALLET (FinaPay)
1A. DEPOSIT MONEY (Add Funds)
"If you want to deposit money, click Add Funds, then choose Bank Transfer, Crypto, or Card. Fill in the details, accept Terms and Conditions, and submit."

Option 1: Bank Transfer
Step 1: Click "Add Funds" button
Step 2: Choose "Bank Transfer"
Step 3: Select which bank account to send to (from list)
Step 4: Enter the amount you want to deposit (in USD)
Step 5: Enter your bank name
Step 6: Enter your account holder name
Step 7: Upload proof of payment (screenshot/receipt)
Step 8: Check the box "I accept Terms and Conditions"
Step 9: Click "Submit"
Step 10: Wait for admin approval (you'll get a notification)
Option 2: Card Payment
Step 1: Click "Add Funds" button
Step 2: Choose "Card"
Step 3: Enter amount you want to deposit (in USD)
Step 4: Click "Continue"
Step 5: Enter your card number
Step 6: Enter expiry date (MM/YY)
Step 7: Enter CVV (3 digits on back)
Step 8: Enter cardholder name
Step 9: Check the box "I accept Terms and Conditions"
Step 10: Click "Pay Now"
Step 11: Complete 3D Secure verification (if asked)
Step 12: Money added instantly to your wallet
Option 3: Crypto Payment
Step 1: Click "Add Funds" button
Step 2: Choose "Crypto"
Step 3: Enter amount in USD
Step 4: Select network (USDT TRC20, BTC, ETH, etc.)
Step 5: Copy the wallet address shown
Step 6: Send crypto from your external wallet to this address
Step 7: Enter the transaction hash
Step 8: Upload screenshot of payment
Step 9: Check the box "I accept Terms and Conditions"
Step 10: Click "Submit Proof"
Step 11: Wait for confirmation (you'll get notification)
1B. BUY GOLD
"If you want to buy gold, click Buy Gold, enter the USD amount, review the gold you'll receive, and confirm."

Step 1: Click "Buy Gold" button
Step 2: Enter how much USD you want to spend
Step 3: See how many grams of gold you'll get (auto-calculated)
Step 4: See the current gold price per gram
Step 5: See any fees applied
Step 6: Check the box "I accept Terms and Conditions"
Step 7: Click "Confirm Purchase"
Step 8: Gold is added to your wallet instantly
1C. SELL GOLD
"If you want to sell gold, click Sell Gold, enter how many grams, see how much USD you'll receive, and confirm."

Step 1: Click "Sell Gold" button
Step 2: Enter how many grams you want to sell
Step 3: See how much USD you'll receive (auto-calculated)
Step 4: See current gold price and any fees
Step 5: Check the box "I accept Terms and Conditions"
Step 6: Click "Confirm Sale"
Step 7: USD is added to your wallet balance
1D. SEND GOLD (Transfer to Friend)
"If you want to send gold to someone, click Transfer, enter their Finatrades ID or email, enter the amount, and confirm."

Step 1: Click "Transfer" or "Send Gold" button
Step 2: Enter recipient's Finatrades ID OR email address
Step 3: Enter amount (grams or USD value)
Step 4: Add a note (optional)
Step 5: Review the transfer details
Step 6: Enter your Transaction PIN (if enabled)
Step 7: Click "Send"
Step 8: Recipient receives gold instantly (or pending if they have approval enabled)
1E. WITHDRAW MONEY
"If you want to withdraw money, click Withdraw, choose your method, enter bank details, enter amount, and submit."

Step 1: Click "Withdraw" button
Step 2: Choose withdrawal method (Bank Transfer)
Step 3: Enter your bank name
Step 4: Enter your account number
Step 5: Enter your IBAN/SWIFT code
Step 6: Enter amount to withdraw (in USD)
Step 7: Review fees and final amount you'll receive
Step 8: Check the box "I accept Terms and Conditions"
Step 9: Enter your Transaction PIN (if enabled)
Step 10: Click "Submit Request"
Step 11: Wait for admin approval and bank transfer
1F. REQUEST GOLD
"If you want to request gold from someone, click Request, enter their details, enter amount, and send the request."

Step 1: Click "Request Gold" button
Step 2: Enter the person's Finatrades ID or email
Step 3: Enter how much gold you're requesting (grams)
Step 4: Add a message explaining why (optional)
Step 5: Click "Send Request"
Step 6: They receive notification and can accept or decline
1G. PENDING TRANSFERS (Accept/Reject)
"If someone sends you gold and you have approval enabled, you'll see pending transfers. You can accept or reject them."

Step 1: Go to Wallet page
Step 2: See "Pending Transfers" section (if any)
Step 3: Review who sent it and how much
Step 4: Click "Accept" to receive the gold
   OR
   Click "Reject" to decline (gold returns to sender)
Step 5: Transfer is processed automatically if you don't respond within 24 hours
2. GOLD STORAGE (FinaVault)
2A. VIEW YOUR STORED GOLD
"If you want to see your stored gold, go to FinaVault and view your holdings with certificates."

Step 1: Click "Gold Storage" in sidebar
Step 2: See your total gold in vault (grams)
Step 3: See individual batches with:
   - Batch reference number
   - Weight in grams
   - Date added
   - Purity (usually 999.9)
Step 4: Click on any batch to see certificate
Step 5: Download certificate as PDF (optional)
2B. DEPOSIT GOLD TO VAULT
"If you want to deposit physical gold to vault, create a deposit request with gold details."

Step 1: Click "Gold Storage" in sidebar
Step 2: Click "New Deposit" tab
Step 3: Enter weight of gold (grams)
Step 4: Enter purity (e.g., 999.9)
Step 5: Enter form type (bar, coin, etc.)
Step 6: Add any notes
Step 7: Click "Submit Request"
Step 8: Follow instructions for physical delivery
Step 9: Admin verifies and approves deposit
Step 10: Gold appears in your vault
2C. WITHDRAW GOLD FROM VAULT (Cash Out)
"If you want to cash out or get physical gold, create a withdrawal request."

Step 1: Click "Gold Storage" in sidebar
Step 2: Click "Cash Out" or "Withdraw"
Step 3: Select which gold batches to withdraw
Step 4: Choose delivery option:
   - Physical delivery (get gold shipped)
   - Sell for cash (convert to USD)
Step 5: Enter delivery address (if physical)
Step 6: Review fees
Step 7: Check terms and conditions
Step 8: Click "Submit Request"
Step 9: Admin processes your request
3. BUY NOW, SELL LATER (BNSL)
3A. CREATE A BNSL PLAN
"If you want to buy gold now and sell it later with guaranteed profit, create a BNSL plan."

Step 1: Click "Buy Now, Sell Later" in sidebar
Step 2: Click "Create New Plan" button
Step 3: Enter how much gold you want to lock (grams)
Step 4: Choose tenor (how many months: 3, 6, 12, etc.)
Step 5: See the guaranteed return % (annual margin)
Step 6: See total payout at maturity
Step 7: Review the agreement terms
Step 8: Type your full name as signature
Step 9: Check "I agree to Terms and Conditions"
Step 10: Click "Confirm & Create Plan"
Step 11: Gold is locked, plan starts
Step 12: Receive payouts as per schedule
3B. VIEW YOUR BNSL PLANS
"If you want to see your active BNSL plans, go to BNSL and view your portfolio."

Step 1: Click "Buy Now, Sell Later" in sidebar
Step 2: See "My Plans" tab
Step 3: View all your plans:
   - Plan ID
   - Gold locked (grams)
   - Status (Active, Completed, etc.)
   - Start date and maturity date
   - Expected payout
Step 4: Click any plan to see full details
Step 5: See payout schedule and history
3C. BNSL WALLET
"Your BNSL earnings go to your BNSL wallet. You can transfer them to your main wallet."

Step 1: Go to "Buy Now, Sell Later"
Step 2: See "BNSL Wallet" card
Step 3: View your BNSL balance
Step 4: Click "Transfer to Main Wallet"
Step 5: Enter amount to transfer
Step 6: Confirm transfer
Step 7: Funds move to your FinaPay wallet
4. TRADE FINANCE (FinaBridge) - For Business
4A. CREATE A TRADE REQUEST (As Importer)
"If you're buying goods internationally and need trade finance, create a trade request."

Step 1: Click "Trade Finance" in sidebar
Step 2: Click "Create Trade Request"
Step 3: Fill in trade details:
   - Goods name and description
   - Quantity
   - Trade value (USD)
   - Expected shipping date
   - Destination country
   - Mode of transport (Sea, Air, Land)
   - Incoterms (FOB, CIF, etc.)
   - Insurance details
Step 4: Review gold collateral required
Step 5: Sign the agreement (type your name)
Step 6: Check Terms and Conditions
Step 7: Click "Submit Request"
Step 8: Exporters can now send proposals
4B. SUBMIT A PROPOSAL (As Exporter)
"If you're a seller and see a trade request you can fulfill, submit a proposal."

Step 1: Click "Trade Finance" in sidebar
Step 2: Browse available trade requests
Step 3: Click on a request to view details
Step 4: Click "Submit Proposal"
Step 5: Fill in your proposal:
   - Quote price
   - Delivery timeline (days)
   - Shipping method
   - Port of loading
   - Payment terms
   - Company details
Step 6: Upload any supporting documents
Step 7: Sign the agreement
Step 8: Check Terms and Conditions
Step 9: Click "Submit Proposal"
Step 10: Wait for importer's response
4C. DEAL ROOM (Negotiate)
"Once proposal is accepted, you enter a Deal Room to chat, share documents, and finalize."

Step 1: Go to "Trade Finance"
Step 2: Click on active trade
Step 3: Enter "Deal Room"
Step 4: Chat with the other party
Step 5: Upload documents:
   - Commercial invoice
   - Bill of lading
   - Certificates
   - Insurance documents
Step 6: Track trade status
Step 7: Confirm shipment and delivery
Step 8: Trade completes, gold is released
5. FINACARD (Coming Soon)
"FinaCard lets you spend your gold balance using a debit card."

Step 1: Click "FinaCard" in sidebar
Step 2: See "Coming Soon" message
Step 3: Register interest (optional)
Step 4: Get notified when available
6. SECURITY SETTINGS
6A. SET UP TRANSACTION PIN
"If you want extra security before transactions, set up a 6-digit PIN."

Step 1: Click "Security" in sidebar
Step 2: Find "Transaction PIN" section
Step 3: Click "Set Up PIN"
Step 4: Enter your account password to verify
Step 5: Enter new 6-digit PIN
Step 6: Confirm PIN (enter again)
Step 7: Click "Save"
Step 8: Now PIN is required before sending money or gold
6B. ENABLE TWO-FACTOR AUTHENTICATION (2FA)
"If you want to protect your login with an authenticator app."

Step 1: Click "Security" in sidebar
Step 2: Find "Two-Factor Authentication" section
Step 3: Click "Enable 2FA"
Step 4: Scan QR code with authenticator app (Google Authenticator, etc.)
Step 5: Enter the 6-digit code from app
Step 6: Save your backup codes (write them down!)
Step 7: Click "Verify & Enable"
Step 8: 2FA is now active for all logins
6C. CHANGE PASSWORD
"If you want to change your password."

Step 1: Click "Security" in sidebar
Step 2: Find "Password" section
Step 3: Click "Change Password"
Step 4: Enter current password
Step 5: Enter new password
Step 6: Confirm new password
Step 7: Click "Save"
7. PROFILE & KYC
7A. COMPLETE KYC VERIFICATION
"To unlock all features, complete your identity verification."

Step 1: Click "Profile" in sidebar
   OR
   Click "Verify Identity" if shown
Step 2: Fill in personal information:
   - Full name
   - Date of birth
   - Address
   - Phone number
Step 3: Upload documents:
   - Government ID (passport, national ID)
   - Proof of address (utility bill, bank statement)
Step 4: Take a selfie for liveness check
Step 5: Click "Submit for Verification"
Step 6: Wait for admin review (usually 1-2 business days)
Step 7: Get notification when approved
8. REFERRAL PROGRAM
"Invite friends and earn rewards when they sign up and trade."

Step 1: Click "Referral" in sidebar
Step 2: See your unique referral code
Step 3: Copy the code or share link
Step 4: Send to friends
Step 5: When they sign up and trade, you earn rewards
Step 6: Track your referrals and earnings
