KB-01 — What Is Finatrades

Title: What is Finatrades?

Finatrades is a gold-backed digital financial platform that allows users to store, transfer, earn, and settle value using physical gold.

Although balances are displayed in USD for convenience, all real value is held in gold grams. Every gram shown on the platform is backed by physical gold stored securely with Finatrades’ vault partner, Wingold & Metals DMCC.

Finatrades combines the reliability of physical gold with the usability of modern digital banking, without relying on fiat inflation or speculative assets.

KB-02 — Gold vs USD (Critical Concept)

Title: Why does my balance show USD if I own gold?

On Finatrades, gold grams are your actual balance.
USD is shown only as a reference value so users can easily understand worth.

Your USD value may change with market prices, but your gold grams remain the same unless you:

Add funds

Send or receive payments

Lock gold in BNSL or trade settlement

Withdraw or sell gold

Title: Personal vs Business Accounts

Personal Account includes:

KB-04 — FinaPay Overview

Title: What is FinaPay?

FinaPay is your gold-backed digital wallet.

You can:

Add funds (Card / Bank / Crypto)

Send payments

Request payments

All transactions represent gold ownership transfers, even though amounts are displayed in USD.

KB-05 — Add Funds Process

Title: How Add Funds Works

Steps:

Enter amount in USD

See equivalent gold grams

Submit payment

Gold grams are credited

After approval, users receive:

Digital Ownership Certificate (Finatrades)

Physical Storage Certificate (Wingold & Metals DMCC)

KB-11 — BNSL Plans

Title: What is BNSL?

BNSL (Buy Now Sell Later) allows users to commit gold for a fixed term and earn a margin.

Plans:

12 months → 10%

24 months → 11%

36 months → 12%

Gold is locked during the term.
Returns accrue daily and are paid quarterly in gold value.

KB-12 — Locked vs Available Balance

Title: Why is my gold locked?

Gold may be locked if:

It is committed to a BNSL plan

It is reserved for a trade settlement (FinaBridge)

Only available gold can be transferred or spent.

KB-13 — FinaBridge Overview

Title: What is FinaBridge?

FinaBridge is a business-only module for gold-backed trade settlement between importers and exporters.

Gold is locked as settlement collateral and released upon trade completion.

KB-14 — Trade Flow (Importer)

Title: Importer Trade Flow

Create trade request

KB-15 — Trade Flow (Exporter)

Title: Exporter Trade Flow
