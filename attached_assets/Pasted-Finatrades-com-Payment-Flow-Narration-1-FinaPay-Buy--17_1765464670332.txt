Finatrades.com – Payment Flow Narration
________________________________________
1. FinaPay – Buy Gold Flow
When a user buys gold through the FinaPay wallet:
1.	The user initiates a Buy Gold transaction inside FinaPay.
2.	The payment amount is debited from the user’s linked funding method (bank card, bank account, or digital wallet).
3.	The corresponding amount is credited to Finatrades treasury/operational account as fiat.
4.	Simultaneously, the purchased gold quantity (in grams) is added to the user’s FinaVault balance.
5.	The system updates:
o	FinaPay → Fiat decreases
o	Finatrades treasury → Fiat increases
o	FinaVault → Gold balance increases for the user
6.	Ownership Status:
o	The user becomes the legal owner of the newly purchased gold, reflected in FinaVault as user-owned allocated gold.
Narration:
“When a user buys gold, the fiat amount is credited to Finatrades, and the equivalent digital gold balance is added to the user’s FinaVault account, establishing the user as the owner of the purchased gold.”
________________________________________
2. FinaPay – Sell Gold Flow
When a user sells gold through FinaPay:
1.	The user initiates a Sell Gold request from FinaPay.
2.	The system reduces (debits) the corresponding gold quantity from the user’s FinaVault balance.
3.	The equivalent fiat value is credited to the user’s FinaPay wallet.
4.	The user can withdraw this amount:
o	to their bank account, or
o	to their crypto wallet (if enabled).
5.	Ownership Status:
o	The user’s ownership of the sold gold is removed, and the gold is no longer allocated to the user in FinaVault.
Narration:
“When a user sells gold, the gold quantity is deducted from their FinaVault balance, ownership is removed, and the equivalent fiat amount is credited to their FinaPay wallet for withdrawal.”
________________________________________
3. Send – Sending Gold to Another FinaPay User
When a user sends gold to another Finatrades user:
1.	The sender selects Send Gold in FinaPay.
2.	The specified gold quantity is deducted from the sender’s FinaVault gold balance.
3.	The same quantity is credited to the receiver’s FinaVault balance instantly.
4.	No fiat movement occurs—this is purely an internal gold transfer on the ledger/Vault system.
5.	Ownership Status:
o	Ownership of the gold moves from the sender to the receiver at the moment of transfer.
Narration:
“Sending gold reduces the sender’s FinaVault balance and increases the recipient’s FinaVault balance in real time, transferring ownership from sender to receiver.”
________________________________________
4. Receive – Receiving Gold From Another FinaPay User
When a user receives gold:
1.	Another user initiates a Send Gold transaction.
2.	The system decreases gold from the sender’s FinaVault balance.
3.	The same amount is increased in the receiver’s FinaVault balance.
4.	Notification is triggered confirming the successful receipt.
5.	Ownership Status:
o	The receiver becomes the new owner of the transferred gold, previously owned by the sender.

Finavault – Narration
________________________________________
1️⃣ Buy Gold – Finavault Changes
• When the user buys gold, the purchased gold quantity is credited to the user’s Finavault balance.
• Finavault increases the user's total gold holdings based on the grams purchased.
• The overall Finatrades gold liability increases by the same quantity.
• A gold credit entry is created in Finavault showing that the user’s gold holdings have increased.
• Ownership Status: The user becomes the rightful owner of the newly added gold.
________________________________________
2️⃣ Sell Gold – Finavault Changes
• When the user sells gold, the selected gold quantity is deducted from the user’s Finavault balance.
• Finavault reduces the user’s total gold holdings accordingly.
• Finatrades’ total gold liability decreases by the same amount.
• A gold debit entry is created in Finavault confirming the deduction of gold from the user's balance.
• Ownership Status: The user’s ownership of that gold is terminated, and the gold is no longer allocated to them.
________________________________________
3️⃣ Send Gold – Finavault Changes
• When a user sends gold to another Finapay user, Finavault reduces the sender’s gold balance.
• The same gold quantity is credited to the receiver’s Finavault balance.
• Total vault gold liability remains unchanged (internal transfer only).
• Finavault records two entries:
•	Debit entry for sender
•	Credit entry for receiver
• Ownership Status: Ownership of the gold shifts from sender to receiver instantly.
________________________________________
4️⃣ Receive Gold – Finavault Changes
• When a user receives gold, the transferred gold quantity is added to the user’s Finavault balance.
• This increase mirrors the deduction already applied to the sender’s balance.
• Total gold liability stays constant because it is only a shift of ownership.
• Finavault records a gold credit entry for the receiving user.
• Ownership Status: The receiver becomes the new legal owner of the transferred gold.
