FinaBridge Deal Room — Latest Features Jo Add Ho Sakti Hain
🔴 Phase 1 — Core Deal Room (Sabse Zaroori)
1. Document Checklist Panel
Har deal ke andar ek structured list jo track kare:

Commercial Invoice
Bill of Lading (BL)
Packing List
Certificate of Origin
Insurance Certificate
Inspection Report
Proof of Lading (POL)
Warehouse Receipt (WR)
Har document ka status: Missing → Uploaded → Under Review → Approved / Rejected

2. Deal Milestone Timeline
Visual progress bar jo dikhaye deal kahan hai:

[✅ Contract] → [✅ LC Issued] → [⏳ Docs Pending] → [ ] Approved → [ ] Settled
3. Discrepancy Management
Documents review karte waqt flag karna ki kya match nahi kiya:

Amount mismatch
Expiry date issue
Port of loading wrong
Missing signature
Description mismatch
4. Deal Summary View
Ek page jisme poora deal ek jagah dikhe — parties, documents, gold amount, timeline, status — bank aur auditor ke liye.

🟡 Phase 2 — Trade Finance Intelligence
5. LC (Letter of Credit) Wizard
Step-by-step form jo importer se LC ke details fill karwaye:

LC type (Irrevocable / Transferable)
Expiry date + place
Amount + currency
Required documents list
Partial shipment allowed ya nahi
Transshipment allowed ya nahi
6. MT700 Field Validator
Jab LC draft upload ho, automatically check kare ki SWIFT MT700 ke mandatory fields sahi hain:

Field 40A, 31D, 32B, 41A, 43P, 44A/B, 45A — sab validate ho
7. Counterparty Risk Dashboard
Har deal mein importer aur exporter ka:

AML risk score
Jurisdiction risk (High / Medium / Low)
Sanctions screening status
KYC completion status
8. Warehouse Receipt (WR) Module
Gold-backed deals ke liye dedicated WR flow:

WR issuance
Endorsement chain track karna
Warehouse inspection status
Release authorization
9. Automated Payment Release
Jab sab documents approve ho jayen → gold automatically release ho importer/exporter ke wallet mein — abhi yeh manual hai.

🟢 Phase 3 — Enterprise & Compliance Grade
10. Dedicated Trade Deal Manager Portal
Admin ke liye dedicated interface:

Assigned deals at a glance
SLA tracking (kitne din ho gaye)
Escalation flag karna
Notes + internal comments
11. Deal Room Analytics

Average deal completion time
Document rejection rate
Most common discrepancies
Active vs closed deals chart
12. Deal Summary PDF Export
Complete deal ka PDF — parties, documents, timeline, gold settlement — banks aur regulators ke liye.

13. Video Verification Call
Importer-Exporter ke beech admin supervised video call directly deal room se — KYC aur identity confirm karne ke liye. (Zoom/Daily.co integration)

14. Smart Notifications

"Document X submitted by exporter — review required"
"LC expiring in 3 days"
"Discrepancy raised on Bill of Lading"
"Deal manager assigned to your trade"
"Gold released — deal complete"
15. Multi-currency Support
Deal room mein AED, USD, EUR sab support karo — gold settlement sab ke liye calculate ho automatically.
