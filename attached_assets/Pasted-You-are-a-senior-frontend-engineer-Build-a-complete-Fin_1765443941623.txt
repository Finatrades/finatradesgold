FINABRIDGE WALLET STRIP
────────────────────────

Directly below top bar, show a **FinaBridge Wallet** row.

This represents a separate wallet layer that sits between FinaPay and Trade Cases.

Two roles:

1) IMPORTER VIEW:
- Card title: "FinaBridge Wallet (Importer)"
- Show:
  - Available Balance: e.g. "250.00 g"
  - Locked for Trade Cases: e.g. "150.00 g"
  - Pending Release: e.g. "0.00 g"
- Secondary text: "Funds here are reserved for trade settlements; they are separate from your FinaPay wallet."
- Button: "Transfer from FinaPay"
  - On click, open modal:
    - Show:
      - "Available in FinaPay: 500.00 g" (mock)
      - Input: "Amount to transfer (g)"
    - On confirm:
      - Decrease FinaPay available balance
      - Increase `finaBridgeWallet.importer.availableGoldGrams`
      - Add audit log entry:
        "Transferred X.XX g from FinaPay to FinaBridge Wallet (Importer)."

2) EXPORTER VIEW:
- Card title: "FinaBridge Wallet (Exporter)"
- Show:
  - Available Balance: e.g. "40.00 g"
  - Incoming Locked Funds: e.g. "150.00 g"
    - Label: "Locked by Importers (Not Yet Released)"
- Secondary text:
  - "Locked funds represent gold reserved by importers for your trade cases. They become available after release."
- No transfer-in from FinaPay here; this wallet is fed by trade releases.

Implementation:
- Use a single component `<FinaBridgeWalletCard role="Importer" | "Exporter" ... />`
- But for now assume one role at a time (e.g., we can toggle role with a mock state).
